Ecosystem health — pattern monitor & treasury

Payout pattern monitor

Behavioral fingerprints of creator payout streams (creators with ≥10 invokes). How to read: Rhythm variety (0–1) — how varied the time gaps between payouts are; humans are irregular (high), cron-job bots are metronomic (low). Repeating pattern (−1–1) — whether the next gap is predictable from the last; near 0 = human, high = scripted rhythm even with random jitter added. Rapid-fire — share of payouts landing within 60s of the previous one; some bursting is normal in a session, extreme values on new wallets suggest farming. Heuristic signals, not determinations — automated invocation can be legitimate in an agentic economy; the automation policy is an open decision. True unique-users-per-skill requires off-chain invoker data (open ask to platform team).
